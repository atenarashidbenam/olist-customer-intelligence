# KPI Definitions

## Overview

This document defines the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) used throughout the Olist Customer Intelligence Platform.

To ensure consistency, every KPI is calculated using standardized business rules implemented in the analytics layer.

Unless otherwise stated, all revenue-related KPIs are calculated **only for delivered orders**.

---

# Revenue

## Definition

The total monetary value of products sold.

## Business Rule

Only delivered orders are included.

Shipping charges are excluded unless explicitly stated.

## Formula

Revenue = SUM(price)

## Used In

- Executive Dashboard
- Monthly Sales
- Product Performance
- Seller Performance

---

# Delivered Orders

## Definition

The total number of successfully delivered orders.

## Business Rule

Only orders with the status **Delivered** are counted.

## Formula

COUNT(DISTINCT order_id)

## Used In

- Executive Dashboard
- Monthly Sales

---

# Average Order Value (AOV)

## Definition

The average revenue generated per delivered order.

## Formula

Average Order Value = Revenue ÷ Delivered Orders

## Used In

- Executive Dashboard
- Monthly Sales
- Seller Performance

---

# Total Customers

## Definition

The number of unique customers who placed delivered orders.

## Business Rule

Customer uniqueness is determined using **customer_unique_id**.

## Formula

COUNT(DISTINCT customer_unique_id)

## Used In

- Executive Dashboard
- Customer RFM

---

# Recency

## Definition

Number of days since the customer's most recent purchase.

## Business Purpose

Measures how recently a customer has interacted with the business.

## Used In

- Customer RFM

---

# Frequency

## Definition

Number of delivered orders placed by a customer.

## Business Purpose

Measures customer purchasing activity.

## Used In

- Customer RFM

---

# Monetary Value

## Definition

Total revenue generated by a customer.

## Business Rule

Only delivered orders are included.

## Used In

- Customer RFM

---

# Units Sold

## Definition

Total number of products sold.

## Formula

COUNT(order_item_id)

## Used In

- Product Performance

---

# Average Selling Price

## Definition

Average selling price of products.

## Formula

SUM(price) ÷ Units Sold

## Used In

- Product Performance

---

# Average Freight Cost

## Definition

Average shipping cost per sold item.

## Formula

AVG(freight_value)

## Used In

- Product Performance

---

# Seller Revenue

## Definition

Total revenue generated by each seller.

## Business Rule

Only delivered orders are included.

## Used In

- Seller Performance

---

# Seller Average Review Score

## Definition

Average customer review score received by a seller.

## Formula

AVG(review_score)

## Used In

- Seller Performance

---

# Average Delivery Time

## Definition

Average number of days between purchase and customer delivery.

## Formula

Delivery Date − Purchase Date

## Used In

- Executive Dashboard

---

# Review Score

## Definition

Average customer satisfaction rating.

## Scale

1 (lowest) to 5 (highest)

## Used In

- Executive Dashboard
- Seller Performance

---

# KPI Design Principles

The project follows these principles for KPI implementation:

- Every KPI has a single authoritative definition.
- Business logic is implemented in SQL views.
- Dashboards consume KPIs directly from the analytics layer.
- Revenue calculations include only delivered orders.
- Customer analysis is based on `customer_unique_id`.
- KPI definitions remain consistent across reports and dashboards.
